Just a quick post to say that I've updated the blog to be a single column layout. 

I was inspired to do so after seeing the minimalist, single column design of [Brent Jackson's blog](https://jxnblk.com/blog), that he made into a [Gatsby Theme](https://www.gatsbyjs.org/docs/themes/introduction/) called [gatsby-theme-mdx-blog](https://github.com/jxnblk/gatsby-theme-mdx-blog).

In the near future, my plan is to follow in Brent's footsteps and try my hand at turning this website into a Gatsby Theme. They are an extremely powerful  abstraction for the [Gatsby ecosystem](https://www.gatsbyjs.org/ecosystem/) (They are still experimental, but highly stable from what I've seen thus far) and I'm looking forward to seeing what pre-built site/app templates I can create with it.

If you're interested in learning more about Gatsby Themes, [Kyle Mathews'](https://www.bricolage.io/) wrote [a great introductory post](https://www.gatsbyjs.org/blog/2019-01-31-why-themes/) on them and [Jason Lengstorf](https://lengstorf.com/) post a great video, helping [Greg Rickaby](https://gregrickaby.dev/) convert his existing Gatsby site into a Gatsby theme, that I've posted below:
